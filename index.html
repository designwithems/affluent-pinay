About Us

Financial literacy implies a mix of mindfulness, knowledge, expertise, attitude, and behavior important to make sound money-related choices and eventually achieve individual financial prosperity.

We are calling to bolster Filipino women's financial literacy by helping them upgrade their knowledge, skills, motivation, and confidence.

What better way to do this than through financial education.

Leading experts in the business, corporate, and financial sectors share their financial proficiency to help Filipino women improve their knowledge of financial concepts, financial products, and financial risks.

By enhancing financial literacy, Filipino women will be more aware of money-related opportunities to make better-informed decisions, know where to go for help, and to take other effective actions to improve their financial well-being.

Mitch Dumlao

Mitch has been providing financial advice to corporate and entrepreneurial moms in Metro Manila and other parts of the country since the year 2000. He believes that putting his clients first and maintaining a high level of professionalism on their behalf is what makes clients eager to refer him to their family and friends.

He brings a high level of expertise to his clients and works diligently with them to find a plan that meets all of their concerns. He's specialty lies in the closing of high-income protection gaps, expensive-illness protection funding, and low-risk but high-yield investment planning.

He has been married for almost 30 years and has two grown sons. He dabbles in digital and online marketing and is passionate about doing sales training, coaching, and marital counseling.
